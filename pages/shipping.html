Shipping Policy

1. Shipping Coverage

GripMaxx currently ships products across India. Delivery availability may vary depending on serviceable pin codes and logistics partner coverage.

2. Order Processing Time

Cash on Delivery orders are typically reviewed and processed within 1-2 business days after they are placed. Orders submitted on weekends or public holidays are processed on the next working day.

3. Delivery Time

Estimated delivery time ranges between 3-7 business days depending on your location. Delivery timelines may vary due to logistics delays, weather conditions, or unforeseen circumstances.

4. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges, if applicable, will be displayed at checkout before completing your purchase. GripMaxx reserves the right to revise shipping fees based on order value or promotional offers.

5. Order Tracking

The current storefront does not provide automated order tracking to customers. For dispatch or delivery questions, customers should contact the store directly using the contact details on the site.

6. Delays & Exceptions

While we aim to deliver within the estimated timeframe, GripMaxx is not liable for delays caused by courier services, natural events, or other circumstances beyond our control.

7. Incorrect Shipping Information

Customers are responsible for providing accurate shipping details. GripMaxx will not be responsible for delays or losses resulting from incorrect or incomplete address information.
